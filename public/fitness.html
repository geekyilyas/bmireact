Fitness Guidance - Exercise Tips

Warm up before exercising to prepare your muscles and reduce the risk of injury. Include dynamic stretches and light cardio.

Vary your workouts to target different muscle groups and prevent plateaus. Incorporate strength training, cardio, and flexibility exercises.

Focus on proper form and technique during exercises to maximize effectiveness and minimize the risk of strains or overuse injuries.
